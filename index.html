COVID statistics and GDP of country analysis

  • Covid was once in a lifetime event which impacted lives of almost entire population across the world.

    The objective of this report is to analyze the impact on lives of residents of different countries and correlate with economic status of these countries.

    As the pandemic progressed, access to medicines and vaccines became critical. And the developed countries did manage to get access to vaccines earlier than not so rich countries. At the same time, several countries adopted different policies with regards to social distancing and vaccination compliance varied across countries.
